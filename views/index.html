What is Cyke?

Cyke lets you improve your recycling habits while helping other people's lives in the process.
We give you tips on how to recycle better and score you based on your how well / much you've been recycling.
You also help donate to charity!
How does Cyke work? Every part of Cyke is essential. You do the most important part: recycle. Recycling plants record when and what you recycle and report that data to us. For giving them a platform, they also give us some percent of their revenue. We tell you how well you're recycling and how you could be better at it. And for every amount of material you recycle, we give you money. You can also choose a to donate some of your earnings to help someone in need.
